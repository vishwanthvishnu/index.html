Iot based college bus tracking and monitoring system

This article proposes an Internet of Things (IoT) based college bus tracking and monitoring system that can be used to track and monitor buses in real time. The system uses sensor modules such as an MQ3 sensor, thermocouple sensor, flame sensor, and PIR sensor. The proposed system will use these sensors to monitor student temperatures, detect fire and smoke in buses, and also the number of students and the health of drivers. All these sensors are connected to an Arduino microcontroller. The data collected by the sensors is sent over wireless networks to a server for storage and analysis. The analyzed data is displayed in the ThingSpeak channel.
